# Privacy Policy for AI Pet Companion

AI Pet Companion ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection
The Extension **does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data**. No user activity, browsing history, or personal information is ever sent to any external server.

## Data Usage
The Extension only uses Chrome's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`) to save your pet selection (e.g., whether you chose "Leisure Dude" or "Goldy Warrior"). This data stays on your device and is never shared.

## Permissions
- **Storage**: Used solely to save your chosen pet.
- **Tabs/Host Permissions**: Used solely to render the pet on the websites you visit.

## Changes
We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

## Contact
If you have any questions, feel free to contact us through our GitHub repository.
